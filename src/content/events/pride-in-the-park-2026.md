Dallas-Fort Worth's biggest outdoor Pride celebration returns to Reverchon Park! Two days of live music, drag performances, food trucks, community org booths, and family-friendly activities.

## Saturday Highlights

- **Main Stage** — Live music and drag performances all day
- **Community Village** — Local LGBTQ+ organizations, resources, and services
- **Family Zone** — Activities and crafts for kids and families
- **Food Court** — Local food trucks and vendors

## Sunday Highlights

- **Brunch & Beats** — Late-morning kickoff with DJs
- **Vendor Market** — Shop queer-owned businesses and artisans
- **Closing Ceremony** — Community awards and celebration

Free admission. Donations welcome and support local LGBTQ+ organizations.
