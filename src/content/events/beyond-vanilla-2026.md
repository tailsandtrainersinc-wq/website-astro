**Beyond Vanilla** is an annual Dallas-based non-profit event bringing world-class workshops and education to the kink community. Whether you're brand new or a seasoned player, BV has something for you.

## What to Expect

- **Workshops** — Hands-on classes on rope, impact play, negotiation, power dynamics, and more
- **Demos** — Live demonstrations from experienced educators
- **Vendor Hall** — Gear, toys, leather, and lifestyle vendors
- **Social Events** — Mixers, play parties, and community connection time
- **Keynote Speakers** — Nationally recognized educators and advocates

## Who Should Attend

Beyond Vanilla welcomes all orientations, identities, and experience levels. The event emphasizes consent culture, safety, and inclusive community building.

Presented in partnership with [NLA-Dallas](https://www.nladallas.org/).
