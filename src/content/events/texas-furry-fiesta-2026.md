**Texas Furry Fiesta** is back for 2026 with the theme **StarPeet!** Join hundreds of fandom members at the Sheraton Dallas Hotel for four days of panels, performances, art, and community.

## Highlights

- **Dealers Den** — Shop art, fursuits, badges, and more from talented creators
- **Artist Alley** — Meet artists and commission work on the spot
- **Art Show** — Browse and bid on original furry artwork
- **Panels & Workshops** — Topics from fursuit-making to creative writing
- **DJ Performances & Dances** — Nightly events to keep the party going
- **Charity Events** — Giving back to causes that matter

## Registration

- Advance registration available at [furryfiesta.org](https://www.furryfiesta.org/attend/registration/)
- At-door rates: $100 (full weekend), $50 (Friday), $60 (Saturday), $40 (Sunday)

## Getting There

The Sheraton Dallas Hotel is in downtown Dallas with easy access from both DFW Airport and Love Field. Hotel room blocks and overflow options are available through the convention website.

Whether you're a longtime furry or just curious, TFF welcomes everyone. Come connect, create, and have fun!
