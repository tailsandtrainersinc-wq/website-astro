This workshop covers the basics of allyship: language, listening, and how to show up without centering yourself.

**What we'll cover:**

- Key terms and why they matter
- How to listen and when to speak
- Supporting someone who comes out
- Taking action in your workplace or school

No prior experience needed. Open to everyone. Light refreshments provided.
