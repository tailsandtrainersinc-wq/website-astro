There are many ways to support Tails and Trainers. Whether you give your time, your voice, or your resources, you help us create a more inclusive world.

## Ways to Help

- **Volunteer** — Help with events, outreach, or behind-the-scenes work. Every hour counts.
- **Donate** — As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we rely on donations to run our programs. No amount is too small.
- **Partner** — Are you part of a business, school, or organization? Let's explore how we can work together.
- **Spread the word** — Follow us, share our work, and tell people about our mission.

## Next Step

The best way to start is to reach out. Tell us what you're interested in, and we'll find a way to get you involved.

We can't wait to hear from you.
